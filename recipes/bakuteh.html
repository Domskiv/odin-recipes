Desription

Bak kut teh, or pork ribs soup, is a popular Chinese dish in Singapore.
The dish consists of pork ribs stewed with a mixture of fragrant herbs and
spices such as garlic, cloves, cinnamon, star anise, fennel seeds and coriander.

Ingredients

  • pork ribs
  • garlic
  • cinnamon
  • star anise
  • fennel seeds
  • coriander
