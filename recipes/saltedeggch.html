Desription

Salted Egg Yolk Chicken is a beloved Singapore and Malaysian zi char restaurant/hawker dish of
fried chicken bites slathered in a creamy, sweet and savoury salted egg yolk sauce. This is also so
incredibly easy to make at home. I used chicken here, but this recipe works with squid and prawns as well.
I made a Salted Egg Yolk Calamari a while back, and this recipe is even easier to make!

Ingredients

  • butter
  • garlic
  • curry leaves
  • bird's eye chillies
  • Evaporated Milk
