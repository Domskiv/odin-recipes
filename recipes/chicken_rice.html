Desription

Bak kut teh, or pork ribs soup, is a popular Chinese dish in Singapore.
The dish consists of pork ribs stewed with a mixture of fragrant herbs and
spices such as garlic, cloves, cinnamon, star anise, fennel seeds and coriander.

Ingredients

  • 3 lb whole chicken(1.3 kg), giblets removed
  • ¼ cup kosher salt(60 g), divided
  • 4 inch pieces fresh ginger, peeled and cut into ¼-inch (6 mm) slices
  • 1 bunch fresh scallion
  • 1 gal cold water(3.7 L), plus more as needed
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
